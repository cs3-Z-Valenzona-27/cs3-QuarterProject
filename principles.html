Principles of Pharmacology

Key principles of pharmacology include understanding the mechanisms of drug action, pharmacokinetics (how the body absorbs, distributes, metabolizes, and excretes drugs), and pharmacodynamics (the effects of drugs on the body).

  • Pharmacokinetics: The study of drug movement throughout the body.
  • Pharmacodynamics: The study of the biochemical and physiological effects of drugs and their mechanisms of action.
