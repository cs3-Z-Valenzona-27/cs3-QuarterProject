Introduction

Pharmacology is the branch of medicine and biology that studies the effects of drugs on the body. It encompasses drug composition and properties, interactions, toxicology, therapy, and medical applications and antipathogenic capabilities.

It is derived from two Greek Words

Pharmakon
which means drug
Logos
which means study

It also involves:

Pharmacokinetics:
the study of how the body interacts with administered substances for the entire duration of exposure.

Pharmacodynamics:
the study of a drug's molecular, biochemical, and physiologic effects or actions.
